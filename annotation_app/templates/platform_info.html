This page includes examples of slurs, coded hate terms, and offensive language so annotators can recognize them accurately.

Platform Context

4chan /pol/: Anonymous politics imageboard where users often rely on irony, slurs, memes, trolling, misspellings, and exaggerated language. Use the full original post, including readable OCR, but do not over-interpret unclear memes or unreadable image text.

Stormfront / Vanguard: White nationalist forums where hate is often framed through news, crime reports, racial grievance, demographic fear, antisemitic conspiracy, and pro-white ideology. Focus on the author's own framing/commentary; do not automatically treat quoted news or quoted replies as the author's stance.

Twitter / X and Gab: Treat the visible post and reply context as the basis for annotation. Some posts use ordinary political language, sarcasm, hashtags, or dog whistles. Use context, but flag cases where the target or meaning is unclear.

Slang and Coded Terms

This list is not comprehensive. If you see an unfamiliar word, search it or flag it, because it may be a slur, coded term, or platform-specific dog whistle.

General / 4chan Style

  • anon / anons: anonymous 4chan users.
  • OP: original poster or original post.
  • kek: lol or mocking laughter.
  • cope / seethe: mocking someone as angry or rationalizing defeat.
  • based / cringe: approval or dismissal.
  • bait: intentionally provocative post.
  • shill, fed, glowie, psyop: accusations of propaganda, government involvement, or manipulation.

Antisemitic / White Nationalist

  • goy / goyim, kike, yid, jewess, Shlomo: terms often used in antisemitic framing; some are slurs.
  • the Jews, ((( ))), ZOG: often signals antisemitic conspiracy claims.
  • globalist, bankers, elites, media owners: may be coded antisemitic depending on context.
  • Holohoax / Holocough: Holocaust denial or minimization.
  • 14/88 / 1488, white genocide: white supremacist codes or demographic-conspiracy framing.
  • mud / muds, race traitor: white supremacist insults.

Anti-Black Terms

  • nigger / nigga, negro, niglet: anti-Black slurs or derogatory terms; context matters, but these are often hate on these platforms.
  • dindu / dindu nuffin: racist caricature implying Black criminality.
  • jogger: coded anti-Black slur.
  • ape, monkey, gorilla: dehumanizing anti-Black terms when race-targeted.
  • Bix Nood / we wuz: mocking African or Black history claims.

South Asian / East Asian

  • pajeet / jeet, paki: slurs for Indian, Pakistani, or South Asian people.
  • curry / currycel, street shitter, bobs and vagene: anti-South Asian insults or tropes.
  • chink, chang, yellow menace: anti-Chinese or anti-East Asian slurs/framing.
  • CCP shill: can be political, but may become anti-Chinese when generalized.
  • bugman: sometimes anti-Asian or anti-modernity insult; context-dependent.

Latino / Immigrant / Muslim / Arab

  • beaner, wetback: slurs for Mexican or Latino people/immigrants.
  • mestizo, third-worlder: often derogatory in white nationalist spaces.
  • illegals / invaders: can be policy language, but often dehumanizing or xenophobic.
  • muzzie, mohammedan, raghead, sand nigger: anti-Muslim, Arab, Middle Eastern, or related slurs.
  • Islamization: can be political, but often anti-Muslim conspiracy framing.

LGBTQ / Gender / Political

  • faggot / fag, tranny, troon: anti-gay or anti-trans slurs.
  • groomer, degenerate, alphabet people: often used to target LGBTQ people.
  • femoid / foid, roastie, thot, whore, slut: misogynistic or gendered sexual insults.
  • feminazi, tradwife: anti-feminist or gender-ideology terms; tradwife is not hateful by itself.
  • MAGA, MIGA, libtard, leftoid, globohomo, antifa: political/factional terms; evaluate whether identity-based hate is present.
  • antitappers: unclear or board-specific; search or flag before labeling.
